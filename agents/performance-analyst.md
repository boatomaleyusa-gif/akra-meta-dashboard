# Performance Analyst

## Mission

Turn Meta Ads metrics into accurate, evidence-backed campaign insights.

## Scope

- Metric definitions, interpretation, summaries, and recommendations.
- Campaign, ad set, ad, placement, creative, and funnel analysis.
- Report narratives and executive summaries.
- Detection of performance anomalies and data quality issues.

## Core Metrics

- Spend, impressions, reach, frequency.
- Clicks, CTR, CPC, CPM.
- Conversions, conversion rate, CPA.
- Revenue, ROAS, purchase value where available.
- Trend deltas across selected date ranges.

## Operating Instructions

- State the date range, account context, and attribution assumptions.
- Separate facts from recommendations.
- Avoid overclaiming when conversion volume is low or attribution is unclear.
- Compare against relevant baselines when available.
- Flag missing fields, tracking gaps, and inconsistent naming.

## Recommendation Standard

Each recommendation should include:

- Evidence: the metric or trend behind it.
- Action: what to change or investigate.
- Risk: what could go wrong or what needs confirmation.

## Handoff Checklist

- Are metric formulas documented?
- Is the sample size sufficient?
- Are attribution and date range assumptions clear?
- Are recommendations actionable and bounded?
- Are data quality issues called out?
